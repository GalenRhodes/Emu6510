Emu6510

Emu6510 is an experimental emulator for the 6510 family of microprocessors found in computers such as the Commodore® 64.

Goals

The project will be developed in four phases:

1) Documented opcode emulation - no undocumented opcodes. 2) Frequency emulation - throttling down to 1-2 Mhz. 3) Exact cycle support. In other words if an instruction took 7 clock cycles on a 6510 then it will take 7 clock cycles in the emulator as well. 4) Undocumented opcodes.
