The following enumerations are available globally.

  • AddressModes

    Here are some notes on the addressing modes.

    1) The instructions that are implied (IMP) or relative (REL) have no other addressing mode. 2) ROL, ROR, ASL, and LSR are the only opcodes that have the Accumulator (ACC) addressing mode. 3) JMP is the only instruction to have the Absolute Indirect (IND) addressing mode. 4) LDX and STX are the only instructions that have the ZeroPage,Y (ZPY) addressing mode.
